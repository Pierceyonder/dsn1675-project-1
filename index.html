About Piercey Design

Joshua Piercey is an illustrator and graphic designer living in Ottawa, Ontario. He has been described as an extremely positive, outgoing, and extroverted person. Aside from graphic design, he is an avid animation junkie who enjoys character design and video gaming and keeping up with technological advancements. His passion lies primarily in illustration, image manipulation, and organizing information into layouts and spreads.
